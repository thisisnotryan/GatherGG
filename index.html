🛡️ Safe Meetup Guide
Gather.gg connects real people. Stay safe.
1
Meet publicly first

For first meetups, choose a public venue — café, community centre, or game shop — before hosting at home.

2
Tell someone your plans

Share who you're meeting, where, and when you expect to be back.

3
Location stays private

Home addresses are only revealed to confirmed RSVPs 24h before. Only your neighbourhood dot shows on the map.

4
Trust your instincts

Cancel your RSVP, block the host, or report the event anytime. We take reports seriously.

5
Under-18 players

Youth events are clearly marked. Age-gated events are enforced at host discretion.
